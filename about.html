About Me

Creative designer and web developer with a love for clean, thoughtful design.

Hi! I’m Madina. I enjoy creating visually balanced work that blends creativity, simplicity, and strong user experience. My portfolio reflects my interests in art, design, and responsive web development.

My Story

I am a creative and detail-oriented designer who enjoys combining artistic ideas with digital design. I am especially interested in clean layouts, gallery-inspired visuals, and building websites that feel both elegant and easy to use.

Through my coursework and projects, I have developed skills in web design, front-end development, and visual communication. I enjoy learning new techniques and applying them in ways that feel creative, intentional, and user-friendly.
